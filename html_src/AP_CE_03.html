Join Korea's most famous live K-POP TV show "Music Bank" on every Friday at 5PM

Who Can Apply for the Concert Ticket?

  • You need to travel to the third country transferring through Incheon International Airport
    (Transit must be completed within 72 hours)
  • You need to be 15 years old and above
  • Korean visa is required if you are non-visa-exempt country citizens

    For more information regarding visa, please visit https://www.visa.go.kr/openPage.do?MENU_ID=1010

How to Get the Concert Ticket

  • 1. Apply for the ticket through the online-booking portal
  • 2. Go to the "Transit Tour Main Desk" at Incheon International Airport and pick up the ticket after the identification check

    Please refer to the map shown below

  • 3. Go to "KBS IBC Hall" and enjoy the show (Tour guide is not available to accompany you to the hall)

    The admission to the hall starts at 4PM and you will be asked to be seated by 4:30PM

How to Get to "Transit Tour Main Desk"

The "Transit Tour Main Desk" is located on the 1st floor at desk number 41&42 between outgoing gates 8&9 on the main terminal building.Working hours are 06:00~18:00

How to Get to "KBS IBC Hall"

Address : 13, Yeouigongwon-ro, Yeongdeungpo-gu, Seoul, Korea (대한민국 서울특별시 영등포구 여의공원로 13)

  • Metro - Incheon Int’l Airport → Gimpo Int’l Airport
    (transfer to Line9) → National Assembly
  • Airport Bus - Terminal 1st floor 6B, 13A →
    Bus(#6030) → National Assembly
