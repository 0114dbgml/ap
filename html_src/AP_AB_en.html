Departure route information

  1. 1. Arrival
    Verify that you will be using Terminal 1. Passengers departing through Terminal 1 should proceed to Floor 3.
  2. 2. Check baggage
    Print boarding pass and check baggage.
  3. 3. Preparation before departuremove
    Complete all business before entering the departure area (including currency exchange, roaming setup, and insurance registration)
  4. 4. Security check
    All passengers must pas through the security check before boarding an aircraft.
  5. 5. Immigration inspectionmove
    Once you pass through the security check and immigration inspection to enter the duty-free zone, you cannot return to the public-accessible area.
  6. 6. Proceed to boarding gate
    Check the gate number and location and proceed
  7. 7. Getting to Terminal 1move
    Passengers using Gates 1-50 board at Passenger Terminal 1
    7. Getting to Concoursemove
    Take the shuttle train from Terminal 1 to the Concourse ※ Once you proceed, you cannot return.
  8. 8. Boarding (Concourse)
    Passengers using Gates 101-132 board at the Concourse
