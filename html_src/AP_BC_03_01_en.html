Baggage Claim

You can easily find your baggage location by checking the baggage carousel number on the billboard.

Baggage process

  1. step 01
    After completing the immigration process, check the Baggage area number.
  2. step 02
    Take the escalators or elevators to Floor 1.
  3. step 03
    Wait by the designated Baggage area.
  4. step 04
    Pick up yours baggage.

    Please verify that the baggage is yours and not someone else’s.

Oversized baggage

  • Proceed to the oversized Baggage area.

Baggage

  • After entry procedure, check your baggage carousel number on the electric notice screen and take the escalator to Floor 1.
