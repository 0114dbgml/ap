Transportation information to get to the Incheon International Airport

Caution when driving to the airport

  • There are separate approach roads for buses and cars(taxis included), so carefully follow the signs to use the correct lanes.
  • For departures, you can get on and off near the closest door to your airline regardless of whether it is a personal car or a taxi. Exception: do not stop for more than 5 minutes.
  • For arrivals, there are separate stopping areas for taxis and personal cars, so follow the signs and stop in correct lanes.
  • Idling or stopping for a long time is not allowed in the departure and arrival areas, so the vehicle must leave immediately after drop off or pick up.
